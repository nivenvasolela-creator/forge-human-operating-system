# Forge: The Human Operating System

I have completed the transformation of Alera from a productivity app into a systemic behavioral loop designed to turn ambitious overthinkers into executing machines.

## The 6-Stage Behavioral Loop

### 1. Capture & Clarify (The Identity Engine)
- **Mind Dump**: Overhauled the onboarding to unload raw thoughts, dreams, and frustrations.
- **AI Extraction**: Updated the background logic to extract **Identity Labels**, **Principles**, and **Standards** from your writing, forging a foundational blueprint.

### 2. Commit (The Workspace)
- **Singular Mission**: Today's screen is now a Workspace. It forces you to define a singular daily mission (win condition).
- **Execution Constraint**: Hard-coded a 3-task limit to kill analysis paralysis.
- **Starting Optimization**: Prioritized the "Begin Focus" button as the only primary decision.

### 3. Control (Silencing the World)
- **Focus Screen**: Created a dedicated, distraction-free environment for deep work.
- **OS Silence**: Entering focus mode automatically hides all navigation and UI noise, forcing you into execution.

### 4. Confront (The Mirror)
- **Execution Gap**: The reflection screen now presents "The Mirror." It calculates your execution rate and shows you the brutal gap between what you planned and what you actually did.
- **Truthful Review**: Focused on three essential questions to "close the slate" for the day.

### 5. Adjust (Invisible Intelligence)
- **Adaptation Engine**: Updated the analysis logic to look for patterns of over-planning and failure.
- **Silence**: Removed all "AI" labels. The system quietly adjusts your load and peak focus windows in the background.
